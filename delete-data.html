Data Management

Delete Your Data

At RUSL-DWUSL, we believe you are in full control of your data. This page explains what data the app stores, where it is stored, and how you can delete it.

✅ You are in control. All game data is stored locally on your device. You can delete it at any time, simply by uninstalling the app.

What Data Is Stored?

  • Your player display name (stored locally on your device)
  • Dispute history — conditions, results, dates (stored locally on your device)
  • Game preferences and settings (stored locally on your device)
  • Temporary multiplayer session data — stored on Firebase servers only during an active game session and automatically deleted when the session ends

How to Delete Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete all data: Uninstall the RUSL-DWUSL app from your device. All local data will be permanently removed.
  • Clear game history: Open the app → go to History → use the clear/delete option within the app.
  • Multiplayer session data: This is automatically deleted from our servers when your game session ends. No action required.

No Account? No Problem.

RUSL-DWUSL does not require you to create an account. We do not store your name, email address, or any personal identifiers on our servers.
