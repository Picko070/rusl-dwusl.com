Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a game?

Open the app, tap "Create Room", set your dispute conditions (what the winner gets and what the loser does), and share the 4-digit room code with your opponent.

How do I join a game?

Tap "Join Room" and enter the 4-digit code your opponent shared with you. If you copied the code, it will be automatically pasted into the input field.

Can I play with someone far away?

Yes! That's the whole point. Share your room code via any messenger — WhatsApp, Telegram, SMS, email — and play from anywhere in the world.

Is the game free?

Yes, RUSL-DWUSL is completely free to download and play.

How do I change the language?

Go to Settings in the app and select your preferred language from the dropdown. RUSL-DWUSL supports 7 languages.

My game history disappeared. What happened?

Game history is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, the history will be lost. Make sure to keep the app installed to preserve your records.
