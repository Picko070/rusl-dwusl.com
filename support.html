Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play RUSL-DWUSL?+
Create a room, set win/lose conditions (like "Winner chooses movie, loser makes popcorn"), share the 4-digit code with a friend, and play Rock-Paper-Scissors. The app determines the winner and saves the result!
Is the game free?+
Yes! RUSL-DWUSL is free to play with ads. You can optionally purchase ad removal or premium features.
Can children play this game?+
Yes! RUSL-DWUSL is suitable for ages 7+. We don't collect personal information, all data is stored locally. Children under 13 should have parental permission.
How is my data protected?+
Game data is stored locally on your device. We use Firebase for multiplayer with minimal data transfer. You can delete all local data anytime through app settings.
What languages are supported?+
English, Deutsch, Español, Français, Italiano, Português, and Русский. More languages coming soon!
Can I play without internet?+
Playing with friends requires an internet connection for real-time multiplayer. However, you can view your game history offline.
How do I report a bug?+
Email us at support@rusl-dwusl.com with details about the issue, your device type, and steps to reproduce. Screenshots are very helpful!
