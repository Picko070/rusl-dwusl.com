Features

Why RUSL-DWUSL?

🎯

Custom Disputes

Set your own win/lose conditions. "Winner picks the movie, loser makes dinner" — you decide the stakes!

🌐

Play Anywhere

Real-time multiplayer via room codes. Share a 4-digit code and play with anyone, anywhere in the world.

🌍

7 Languages

English, Deutsch, Español, Français, Italiano, Português, Русский — play in your language.

📜

Dispute History

Every dispute is saved. Go back and check who won, what the conditions were, and settle the score.

🏆

Best of 3 or 5

Choose single round, best of 3, or best of 5. Higher stakes = more drama!

🔒

Private & Secure

Game data stays on your device. No accounts needed, no personal data collected.

How It Works

Four Simple Steps

1

Create a Room

Set your dispute conditions — what the winner gets, what the loser does.

2

Share the Code

Send the 4-digit room code to your opponent via any messenger.

3

Play!

Both players pick Rock, Paper, or Scissors — results revealed simultaneously.

4

Dispute Resolved!

Winner and loser announced. Result saved to history. Fair and square!
