Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

PocketClub LLC ("PocketClub", "we", "us", or "our") operates the RUSL-DWUSL mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. RUSL-DWUSL is designed to collect as little personal data as possible.

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Player display names (stored locally on your device only)
  • Game dispute conditions and history (stored locally on your device only)

Information collected automatically:

  • Anonymous authentication tokens (via Firebase Authentication) — these do not contain personal information such as your name, email, or phone number
  • Game session data (room codes, player choices) — temporarily stored on our servers during active gameplay and automatically deleted after the session ends
  • Basic device and usage information for app functionality and crash reporting

How We Use Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Enable multiplayer gameplay between devices
  • Maintain game sessions and synchronize game state
  • Improve app stability and fix bugs

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Game history and player preferences are stored locally on your device using standard device storage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Multiplayer session data is temporarily stored on Google Firebase servers (located in the United States) and is automatically removed after the game session ends.

We implement reasonable security measures to protect the data processed through our services.

Third-Party Services

RUSL-DWUSL uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase — for anonymous authentication and real-time multiplayer functionality. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy

We may integrate additional third-party services (such as advertising networks) in the future. This policy will be updated accordingly before any such integration.

Children's Privacy

RUSL-DWUSL is rated for all ages (4+ on App Store, Everyone on Google Play). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since our app uses anonymous authentication only, no personal data such as names, emails, or locations is collected from any user, including children.

Your Rights

Since game data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can delete your game history at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data.

If you have any questions about your data, you can contact us and we will respond promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
